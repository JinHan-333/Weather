How the visualization
works

The "Living Archive" is built on the principle of poetic translation. We don't just plot points on a grid; we map the internal friction of the student commute to atmospheric disturbances.

Each time a user submits a check-in, our algorithm interprets the numerical values through a series of visual metaphors. Weather provides the background texture—the "canvas" state. Commute difficulty distorts the paths of these elements, mirroring the literal and mental twists of a rough journey.

Stress levels define the density of visual noise, while mood acts as a global light source, illuminating or dimming the collective experience.
